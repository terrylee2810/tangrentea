Ang Ku Kueh

Ji Xiang Confectionery has been specializing in handmade Ang Ku Kueh since 1988. Ang Ku Kueh is a traditional Chinese snack with its origin from Fujian, China. Ang Ku Kueh literally means "Red Turtle Cake" in Hokkien, which symbolizes Prosperity, Longevity and Wealth. Ang Ku Kueh is typically used for praying, festive and special events purposes. Our Ang Ku Kueh comes in eight fillings - Peanut, Sweet Bean, Savoury Bean, Corn, Coconut, Yam, Durian and Seasame, ranging between $1.00 to $2.50 each. Durian Ang Ku Kueh is seasonal depending on durian availability. Monday to Friday - all 8 fillings available, Saturday - Peanut and Sweet Bean only. 2 days advance notice, minimum order 10pcs/order.
